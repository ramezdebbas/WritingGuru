Writing Guru:
Description:
Writing Guru is an app where you can find the latest ways & direction for how to do creative writing, here are some important directions to learn about creative writing.

Features:
•	Fully research based app on How to learn about creative writing.
•	The Research results on the How to write in a creative way.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
